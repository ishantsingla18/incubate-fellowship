ABOUT US

INCIND Technology Fellowship is a unique and exclusive program for the coders, hackers, designers and innovators of tomorrow. As part of this fellowship, you will get an opportunity to develop your skills through working with senior technology leaders and industry experts.



Over the years, a lot of talented students seeking opportunities to sharpen their skills have joined IncubateIND to get to work with industry leaders and to understand the complexities of the tech world. This Fellowship will make students work with technology leaders, and it aims to improve the chances of a few selected students to get hired by their dream organizations in the future.





BENEFITS

Become a Community Leader:

Build you own community chapter for your campus with innovators under the IncubateIND Fellowship Program.

Exclusive Internship & Job Opportunities:

Priority access to internship & job opportunities with top tech startups hosted at IncubateIND Jobs and UPRISE

Industry Connect and Networking:

A golden opportunity to meet tech wizards and pioneers in various fields.

Expert Mentorship:

Get exposure to numerous industry practices that will help you to become a great leader/developer.

Real-world Projects:

Work with the Internal team of IncubateIND on various industry projects and events which will help you gain insights about the industry and great experience.

Compensation:

Certificates, goodies, and many more.

Tech-Exposure:

Fellows would get to participate in and publicize various technology based events, this will help gain exposure.

Other exciting events:

You can participate in many of IncubateIND's organized hackathons, knowledge sessions, boot-camps and many technology events.
